I am Juan Ortega, a Bachelor of Science in Physics and a current Ph.D. student in Engineering Sciences with a specialization in Mechanical Engineering at the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. 

I conduct my research at The Hidden Heart Lab, under the supervision of Professor Francisco Sahli Costabal. My work is focused on the intersection of computational mechanics, cardiac electrophysiology, and Scientific Machine Learning (Scientific ML).

My main goal is to develop efficient computational models that combine classical physics with neural networks (PINNs) to improve the simulation and understanding of cardiac pathologies.

Areas of Interest

  • Scientific Machine Learning: Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs).
  • Cardiac Modeling: Eikonal Equation, anisotropic tensors, fluid dynamics.
  • Scientific Computing: Development of differentiable solvers in Python (JAX) and MATLAB.
